Geography bern

short description of bern

  • Location

    Bern is located on the Swiss Plateau, in the canton of Bern, slightly west of the country’s center and about 20 km north of the Bernese Alps.

  • Landscape Formation

    The surrounding landscape was shaped by glaciers during the last Ice Age.

  • Nearby hills

    Two notable hills close to the city are:

    1. the Gurten (858 m),
    2. the Bantiger (947 m).

  • Urban topography

    The city initially developed on a peninsula formed by a meander of the River Aare, which limited its natural expansion until the 19th century, when bridges allowed it to spread beyond the river.

  • Altitude differences

    Bern’s urban terrain is quite irregular: there are altitude differences of several dozen meters between districts close to the river (such as Matte and Marzili) and higher ones (Kirchenfeld, Länggasse).
